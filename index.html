Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement in my opinion makes total sense. Essentially you are separating your web development into parts. First HTML, Next CSS, and last your Java Script. To me this is a good idea because it creates step-based system. You don't bake a cake using every ingredient at one time, you split it into parts. With progressive enhancement you focus on the content and eventually get to the decoration. Content first means you aren't losing any of your valuable information. The information is the most important part of a webpage. Why else would someone want to use it anyway? No one goes to a website to see how pretty the words are or how pretty the animations are. In my opinion Progressive Enhancement makes the most sense when building your webpage.
